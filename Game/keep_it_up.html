Can you help Johnny keep it up!?!?!?

How many juggles can you get? Put your soccer skills to the test!

How to Play:

  • First select which level you wish to play from the dropdown menu found to the left of the game window. The higher the leve, the more juggles are needed to win!
  • Then, click the "Start" button. Your character and a soccer ball will appear on the screen moments later.
  • Use the left and right arrow keys to move your player under the bouncing soccer ball in order to use your character's head to keep the ball up!
  • Be careful not to let the ball drop below your player's head!
  • Head the ball as many times as is required based on your level selection in order to win!
  • You can check the boxes to the left for some powerups that will help you beat the levels!
